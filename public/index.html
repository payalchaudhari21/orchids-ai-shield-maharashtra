Process Flow

System Workflow

End-to-end process from detection to resolution

  1. 01

    User Submission

    Citizen uploads suspicious media through web portal, mobile app, or WhatsApp bot. System accepts images, audio, video, and text.

    Web Portal Mobile App WhatsApp
  2. 02

    Pre-Processing

    Media is validated, compressed if needed, and prepared for analysis. Metadata extraction and format normalization occurs.

    Validation Metadata Normalization
  3. 03

    AI Analysis

    Multiple neural network models analyze the content simultaneously. Deep learning algorithms detect manipulation signatures.

    CNN Transformer GAN Detection
  4. 04

    Confidence Scoring

    Results from multiple models are aggregated. Ensemble voting produces final authenticity score with confidence interval.

    Ensemble Scoring Validation
  5. 05

    Result Delivery

    User receives detailed report with verdict, confidence level, and recommended actions. Available in English and Marathi.

    Report Multi-language Guidance
  6. 06

    Authority Alert

    High-risk content automatically flagged to Cyber Cell. Case tracking initiated for serious threats.

    Auto-Alert Case Tracking Response

üö® Red Flags to Watch For

üìû

Urgent Family Calls

Scammers use cloned voices claiming emergencies. Always verify through a separate call.

üí∞

Money Requests

Any request for immediate money transfer via call or message should be verified in person.

üèÜ

Too Good to Be True

Lottery wins, job offers, or prizes you didn't apply for are almost always scams.

üîó

Suspicious Links

Never click links from unknown sources. Check URLs carefully for misspellings.

üë§

Unnatural Images

Look for blurred edges, inconsistent lighting, or strange artifacts in photos.

üéôÔ∏è

Robotic Voice

AI voices may have subtle glitches, unnatural pauses, or monotone patterns.

No Bias

üìã

Transparency

Complete disclosure of how our AI makes decisions. Explainable AI outputs for every analysis.

ü§ù

Human Oversight

Critical decisions always reviewed by human experts. AI assists but doesn't replace human judgment.

‚ö†Ô∏è Known Limitations

üìä

Accuracy Rate

While our system achieves 95% accuracy, no AI system is perfect. Results should be considered indicators, not absolute verdicts.

üÜï

New Techniques

AI generation technology evolves rapidly. Very new techniques may not be detected until our models are updated.

üñºÔ∏è
